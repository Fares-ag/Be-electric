## üìã Creating Work Orders

### Method 1: QR Code Scanning (Recommended)

1. **Tap the "+" button** on the dashboard
2. **Tap "Scan QR Code"**
3. **Point your camera** at the asset's QR code
4. **Wait for scan** - asset information will auto-populate
5. **Fill in the form**:
   - Problem Description: Describe the issue
   - Repair Category: Select appropriate category
   - Priority: Choose urgency level
   - Photo: Attach photo if needed
6. **Tap "Submit Request"**

### Method 2: Manual Asset Search

1. **Tap the "+" button** on the dashboard
2. **Tap "Search Asset"**
3. **Enter search terms**:
   - Asset name
   - Asset ID
   - Location
4. **Select from results**
5. **Complete the form** as above

### Repair Categories

Choose the most appropriate category:

- **Mechanical & HVAC**: AC, ventilation, elevators, lifts
- **Electrical**: Power outlets, wiring, lighting systems
- **Structural**: Foundation cracks, roof leaks, building repairs
- **Plumbing & Water**: Water supply, drainage, pumps
- **Interior**: Ceiling tiles, flooring, painting
- **Exterior**: Pavement, landscaping, facade
- **IT & Low Voltage**: Network, CCTV, intercom systems
- **Specialized Equipment**: Lifts, machinery, kitchen tools
- **Safety & Compliance**: Fire safety, safety barriers
- **Emergency**: Electrical outages, burst pipes
- **Preventive**: Scheduled maintenance
- **Reactive**: Fixing after failure

### Priority Levels

- **Low**: Non-urgent, can wait
- **Medium**: Standard priority
- **High**: Important, needs attention soon
- **Critical**: Urgent, requires immediate action

## üîß Managing Work Orders

### For Technicians

#### Viewing Your Tasks

1. **Go to "Work Orders"** from the dashboard
2. **View assigned tasks** in the list
3. **Use filters** to organize by status or priority
4. **Tap any work order** to view details

#### Starting Work

1. **Open the work order** from the list
2. **Review the details**:
   - Asset information
   - Problem description
   - Priority and category
   - Attached photos
3. **Tap "Start Work"** when you begin
4. **Travel to the asset location**

#### Completing Work

1. **After fixing the issue**, tap "Complete Work"
2. **Fill out the completion form**:
   - **Corrective Actions**: What you did to fix it
   - **Recommendations**: Suggestions to prevent future issues
   - **Next Maintenance**: When it should be checked again
   - **Notes**: Any additional information
3. **Capture signatures**:
   - **Requestor Signature**: If the person who reported it is present
   - **Technician Signature**: Your signature
4. **Tap "Close Ticket"** to complete

### For Managers

#### Viewing All Work Orders

1. **Go to "Work Orders"** from the dashboard
2. **View all work orders** in the system
3. **Use filters** to organize by:
   - Status (Open, Assigned, In Progress, Completed)
   - Priority (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
   - Category (Mechanical, Electrical, etc.)
   - Technician

#### Assigning Work

1. **Find an open work order**
2. **Tap to open details**
3. **Tap "Assign"**
4. **Select a technician** from the list
5. **Confirm assignment**

#### Monitoring Progress

- **Dashboard**: Overview of all work
- **Work Order List**: Detailed view of all tasks
- **Filters**: Organize by status, priority, or technician
- **Statistics**: Track completion rates and performance

## üìÖ Preventive Maintenance

### Viewing PM Tasks

1. **Go to "PM Tasks"** from the dashboard
2. **View scheduled tasks** by due date
3. **Overdue tasks** are highlighted in red
4. **Use filters** to organize by status or technician

### Completing PM Tasks

1. **Select a PM task** from the list
2. **Review task details**:
   - Asset information
   - Task description
   - Checklist items
   - Due date
3. **Perform the maintenance work**
4. **Check off checklist items** as you complete them
5. **Add completion notes** if needed
6. **Capture your signature**
7. **Mark as completed**

### PM Task Types

- **Daily**: Tasks that need to be done every day
- **Weekly**: Tasks scheduled weekly
- **Monthly**: Monthly maintenance tasks
- **Quarterly**: Quarterly inspections
- **Annually**: Annual maintenance and inspections

### Common Issues

#### App Won't Start

- **Restart the app**
- **Check device storage**
- **Update the app** if available
- **Contact IT support**

#### Can't Scan QR Codes

- **Clean camera lens**
- **Ensure good lighting**
- **Hold device steady**
- **Try manual search** instead

- **Check camera permissions**
- **Ensure device storage space**
- **Try taking a new photo**
- **Restart the app**

#### Data Not Syncing

- **Check internet connection**
- **Go to Settings** ‚Üí "Sync Status"
- **Try manual sync**
- **Contact IT support**

#### Can't Login

- **Check email and password**
- **Ensure caps lock is off**
- **Try resetting password**
- **Contact system administrator**
